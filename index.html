About

Technology has drastically changed the way we think and form ideas, communicate and interact with others, make decisions and create solutions for local and world-wide issues. As a community organizer in the Bay Area, I realized the power technology has in creating solutions for our most pressing issues. I became inspired by the relationship between technology and community, making lives just and equitable for everyone.

As a social worker, I believe that we are self-determined architects of our lives, and sometimes we have to use the roads less taken. I have arrived at an intersection, leveraging the same skill sets that I have gained from my 7+ year professional career, combined with my newfound programming skills to make communities more conscious in all areas of life.

Since making this transition I’ve developed a number of projects, ranging from designing and building both the database and user interface for a web app for storing information on my favourite movies, to creating a simple Javascript app to search for, and learn about specific Pokemon capabilities. I am determined to contionue to grow as a web developer, and embrace all new techniques and technologies in the days to come.
